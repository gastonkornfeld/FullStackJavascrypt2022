DOCTOR STRANGE

Formerly a renowned surgeon, Doctor Stephen Strange now serves as the Sorcerer Supreme Earth’s foremost protector against magical and mystical threats.

Strange begins to master the mystic arts under the tutelage of the Ancient One, aided by Mordo.

Doctor Strange uses the Eye of Agamotto’s power to force Dormammu into a bargain to save the Earth.

When Thor comes looking for his father, Odin, on Earth, Doctor Strange offers him assistance.

On Titan, Doctor Strange joins several other heroes in a battle against Thanos, attempting to remove the Infinity Gauntlet from his hand.
